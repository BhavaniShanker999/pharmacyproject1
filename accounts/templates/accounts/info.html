Site Explanation:

This is Pharmacy medicines delivery website. The delivery agency is a third party sector where it takes the medicine orders, from Pharmacutical Labs and deliver to the corresponding paharamcies according to the list given by the Labs. So this third-party delivery agency manages all the clients orders under their respective client names and look after the deliveries. After delivering the medicine package to their respective paharamcies, we mark the order in our website delivered if not so it is marked pending/Out for delivery. Thus it keeps the track and record of all the Orders of clients here with ease to see their info whenever needed 24X7. This enables the delivery-agency for fast upadtes and info about their deliveries getting rid of bulk papers.

1.What Does this site do?

This site is meant to keep track and record of the products to be delivered[ Here for example i used the pharmacy].

2.Where it can be used?

It can be used in Deivery,Logistics anywhere, where a person has to deliver the products to customers/shops and keep a record of them and access at anytime instead carrying and storing them manually.
